Acodeidea Joins Sikich

Coimbatore, India — August 21, 2023 — Acodeidea, a software company headquartered in Coimbatore with premises in FORGE Accelerator, is delighted to announce its team joining Sikich.


Sikich LLP, a global tech-focused firm, provides a range of tech solutions for impactful digital strategies. A top US CPA firm, Sikich serves diverse clients—corporations, nonprofits, governments—with services and products that enhance performance and achieve strategic goals.


Sikich LLP, a global tech-focused firm, provides a range of tech solutions for impactful digital strategies. A top US CPA firm, Sikich serves diverse clients—corporations, nonprofits, governments—with services and products that enhance performance and achieve strategic goals.


This partnership marks a pivotal moment for both entities, as they synergize their expertise to drive forward digital transformation and innovation across highly regulated industries, characterized by technological advancement and operational excellence.


Cultivating a prolonged connection, Acodeidea has remained a steadfast advisory associate to Sikich's Regulatory, Quality, and Compliance (RQ&C) sector, emphasizing Quality Management Systems (QMS) and Regulatory Validation within the confines of 21CFR Part11 stipulations. Today, Acodeidea takes pride in formalizing its inclusion within the Sikich fold, laying the groundwork for a dynamic distribution structure.


“Since our inception, we've nurtured the culture of accountability and autonomy with remarkable team perks for all our members. As we join Sikich,these values persist seamlessly." says Vidya Ravichandran, Acodeidea's Director of Operations.


The team stands at the forefront of propelling digital excellence in regulated sectors, backed by deep proficiency. This seasoned expertise is set to reshape norms and deliver inventive solutions.


Naveen Krishnamurthy, Director of Technology Services at Acodeidea, articulated, "Over time, our consultancy has seamlessly integrated into Sikich's RQ&C vertical as a trusted partner. We've dedicated ourselves to QMS, Regulatory Validation and LegalTech implementations."


The recently integrated Sikich team will operate from Coimbatore, India, maintaining their presence. This establishes Sikich's third India location, following Ahmedabad and Bengaluru.
